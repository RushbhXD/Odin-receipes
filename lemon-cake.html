Simple Lemon Cake

This simple lemon cake is perfect for picnics and parties. It's moist and soft.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
